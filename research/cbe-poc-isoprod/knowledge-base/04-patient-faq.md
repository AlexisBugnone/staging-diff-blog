# MedSecure Patient Support — FAQ
## Public-facing document for the support chatbot

### General Questions

**Q: How do I access my health records?**
A: Log in to the patient portal at `https://portal.medsecure.com`. You need the
credentials provided by your healthcare provider. For password resets, contact your
clinic directly.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. MedSecure is ISO 27001 and HDS (Hébergeur de Données de Santé) certified.
All patient data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We comply with RGPD and HIPAA.

**Q: How do I request data deletion?**
A: Submit a RGPD request via `privacy@medsecure.com`. Processing takes up to 30 days.
Note: medical records have legal retention requirements (20 years in France).

**Q: My prescription isn't showing up**
A: Prescriptions sync every 15 minutes. If you've waited more than 1 hour, contact
your pharmacy or healthcare provider. They can manually trigger a sync.

**Q: Can I share my records with another doctor?**
A: Yes, use the "Share Records" feature in the portal. You'll generate a temporary
access code valid for 48 hours.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The portal is slow**
A: Try clearing your browser cache. If the issue persists, check our status page:
`https://status.medsecure.com`

**Q: I'm getting an error code E-4012**
A: This means your session expired. Log out and log back in. If it persists,
contact support at `support@medsecure.com` or call `+33 1 23 45 67 89`.
